Submission

Submit before Jan 10, 2017

All talk submissions will be anonymized before the selection committee sees them.

Topics

Does it involve programming? Are you excited by something that you created? Can you explain something that you learned? Does it bring you joy? We want to hear about it!

If you know how an algorithm/tool/system/language works under the hood, teach us!

If your experience of learning/practicing programming is different from the mainstream, share it with us!

If you are concerned with the cultural/ethical/social implications of a programming topic, help us understand!

Style

Whatever helps your audience understand your topic, without exceeding the 10 minute limit.

A microphone and video projector will be provided by default.

Why Submit?

First time speaker? Old hand at proposing talks? We'd love to include you!

  • Nobody knows your topic better than you.
  • 10 minutes is just enough time to get excited.
  • Programming is fun, and so are you!
